Academic Support at UMSI

At the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI), we are committed to supporting our students' academic success by offering a wide array of resources and services. Our academic advising team provides personalized guidance to help you navigate your curriculum, select courses, and address any academic challenges. UMSI students also benefit from extensive study spaces and collections available in the University Library system, alongside tutoring services and the UMSI Writing Center.

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

Mental health is a crucial part of your well-being. The CAPS program offers comprehensive mental health support in a safe and supportive environment.

Services Offered

  • Individual and group counseling sessions.
  • Workshops on stress management and mindfulness.
  • 24/7 crisis intervention services.

Learn more by visiting the CAPS website.

How to Access CAPS

To schedule an appointment, call CAPS or use their online scheduling system. Confidential support is always available to assist you.
